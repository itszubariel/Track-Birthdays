1 Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Track Birthdays, we collect:

  • Account information — email address, username, and password (stored securely, never in plain text)
  • Profile information — full name, profile photo, and your own birthday if you choose to provide them
  • Birthday data — names, dates, notes, and photos of people whose birthdays you add
  • Notification preferences — your preferred notification time
  • Push subscription data — browser push tokens used to deliver notifications to your devices

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the app's features:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your birthday data
  • Send push notifications about upcoming birthdays
  • Allow you to customise your profile and preferences

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Profile photos and avatars are stored using Supabase Storage.

4 Third-Party Services

We use the following services solely to operate the app. Your data is shared with them only to the extent necessary:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — background job scheduling for notifications (Privacy Policy)
  • Google FCM — push notification delivery for Chrome and Brave (Privacy Policy)
  • Mozilla Push Service — push notification delivery for Firefox (Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify — web hosting (Privacy Policy)

We do not sell your data to any third party.

5 Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, your browser generates a push subscription token stored in our database. This token is used exclusively to deliver birthday reminders. You can disable notifications at any time from your browser settings or profile page.

6 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, birthday entries, and push subscriptions — is permanently deleted.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information at any time via your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data via the profile page
  • Disable push notifications at any time

8 Children's Privacy

Track Birthdays is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10 Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out at contact.trackbirthdays@gmail.com.
